About

I am a researcher working in natural language processing, multilingual language technologies, language modeling, and corpus linguistics. My research focuses on data-driven approaches to language analysis and modeling.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Translation
  • Multilingual Language Models
  • Language Resources for Minority Languages
  • Information Extraction
  • Corpus Linguistics
